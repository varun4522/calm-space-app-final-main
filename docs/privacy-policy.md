# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 23, 2026**

Calm Space ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use the Calm Space mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Profile Information
When you register, we collect your name, email address, registration number, course, and phone number.

### User Content
Journals, mood logs, and messages shared within the app are stored to provide app functionality.

### Location Data
We collect your precise location (GPS coordinates) **ONLY** when you explicitly use the "Share My Location" feature in the Emergency tab. This data is collected to facilitate emergency assistance. We do **NOT** collect your location in the background.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide mental wellness support and resources.
- To facilitate emergency assistance by sharing your coordinates with designated responders.
- To display your mood trends and journaling history.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Emergency Responders
Your location is shared with authorized responders during an active emergency request.

### Service Providers
We use Supabase (a backend-as-a-service) to store your profile, location history, and app data.

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law.

## 4. Your Rights and Data Deletion

- You can view and edit your profile information in the Settings screen.
- You can delete your entire account and all associated data via the "Delete Account & Data" button in Settings. This action is permanent and irreversible.

## 5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including Supabase's secure authentication and encryption, to protect your data.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at support@calmspace.com.
